A drawing is a [[Drawing/Preliminaries/Projection|Projection]] of a 3D scene on a surface.

There are two variables that we can play with:
1. **The surface**
	- a plane
	- a sphere
	- a cube
	- any other surface
2. **The type of projection**
	- along parallel lines -> Isometric/Axonometric projection
	- along radial lines -> Central projection

And combining these results in some familiar things:
- Isometric Projection + Plane => Isometric/Axonometric Drawing. Used in architecture drawings or in CAD.

- Central Projection + Plane => 1, 2, 3 point perspective (linear perspective)
- Central Projection + Sphere => 4, 5 point perspective (curvilinear perspective)
> Why? Because that's what vision is. A Central Projection (light rays going in) on the inside of a Sphere (the eye).

 - Central Projection + Cube => Video Game Skybox
